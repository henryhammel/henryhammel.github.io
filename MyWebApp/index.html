About the Guide

About the Project:

Our data will be focused on 41 of the Best Things To Do & Places to Visit In New Mexico, this data will provide a visual representation of the data within an article published by BUSYTOURIST that was updated this January. This website provides a brief background on the attraction and ideal dates to visit. New Mexico Top 25 Attractions was updated this year by Attractions of America. This website can provide additional data on the locations we will be focusing on. This site provides the reader with information on places to stay in each area and other articles that mention the area.
